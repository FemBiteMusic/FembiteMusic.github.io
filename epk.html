Band Overview

Bio

Hailing from a planet where girls are tall and boys are small, Fembite enters your orbit like a caffeinated crash out with fast cracking drums and high octane guitar. These emo pop girlies will tear bites out of gender heartbreak and spit out cheeky lyrics that make your heart scream.
