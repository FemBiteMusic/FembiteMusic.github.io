Short Bio

Queer rock. Tall girl/small girl pop. Loud feelings and soft rebellion from Victoria, B.C.

Long Bio

Soft but ferocious, Fembite is a queer pop-punk band based in Victoria, B.C., made up of Lauren Odds on lead vocals and rhythm guitar, Ariel Tseng (of Fortune Killers and Isobel Trigger) on drums, Sienna Waibel on lead guitar and Robin Melville on bass. Our music blends the energetic guitar styles of Paramore and MCR with the vulnerable lyric style of Olivia Rodrigo. Rooted in our personal and collective experiences, our songs will bring you rebellious energy to help you forget about all your problems. â€‹ We make music we wish we had growing up and hope to help others feel seen too.
