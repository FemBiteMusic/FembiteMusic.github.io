About Fembite

Fembite is a queer pop-punk band based in Victoria, B.C. Their music blends the energetic guitar styles of Paramore and MCR with the vulnerable lyric style of Olivia Rodrigo. Fembite is made up of Lauren Odds on lead vocals and rhythm guitar, Ariel Tseng (of Fortune Killers and Isobel Trigger) on drums, Sienna Waibel on lead guitar and Robin Melville on bass. Rooted in their personal and collective experiences, their rebellious energy will help you forget about all your problems. ​
